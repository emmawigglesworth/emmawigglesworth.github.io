Project spotlight

Arcade stories crafted in Pygame

A collection of kinetic prototypes exploring arcade physics, playful feedback, and how to build satisfying game feel with lightweight Python tools.

Designing delightful mechanics

Iterated on jump arcs, enemy behaviour, and level pacing to keep every encounter feeling dynamic and fair. Quick playtests informed micro-adjustments to speed, friction, and collision so the controls respond instantly.

Modular systems

  • Entity-component architecture for re-usable enemies and power-ups.
  • Scene manager to swap game states without duplicating code.
  • Particle + audio hooks for juicy hits, wins, and level transitions.

Learning outcomes

Rebuilt my understanding of Python best practices, prototyping loops, and performance on modest hardware. The toolkit now powers weekend jams and experimentation with friends.
