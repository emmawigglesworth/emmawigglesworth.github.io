Project spotlight

Bin There Operations Hub

I partnered with Bin There, a Toronto-based startup, to digitise their logistics workflow. The web app coordinates customer orders, container inventory, and driver routes in one place, helping the team scale beyond spreadsheets.

Understanding the business

I joined site visits, shadowed dispatchers, and mapped the end-to-end service journey to identify pain points. Those insights informed user stories and guided each release.

Product delivery

  • Built scheduling views with React and Tailwind that visualised container availability in real time.
  • Connected a Node.js backend to MongoDB to track jobs, invoices, and customer profiles.
  • Set up automated reminders and status updates so clients always knew what to expect.

Result

Dispatch time dropped significantly because teams could see the entire day at a glance, while leadership gained reliable reporting for growth planning. The engagement also gave me hands-on experience leading a project end-to-end with a small business.
