Let's talk

Creative lab

Animation studies & motion systems

A playground for exploring timing, anticipation, and story through 2D illustration and 3D motion experiments.

Principles in practice

Daily sketch cycles focused on squash & stretch, overlap, and expressive posing. These studies feed directly into UI micro-interactions and in-game cutscenes.

Tools & techniques

  • Blender and Grease Pencil for stylised 3D / 2D hybrid workflows.
  • Procreate + After Effects for cel-inspired loops and GIF experiments.
  • Shader Graph to add procedural motion and dynamic lighting.

Sharing the work

Curated selections are showcased at campus showcases and online galleries. Each study is annotated with process notes so collaborators can learn alongside me.
