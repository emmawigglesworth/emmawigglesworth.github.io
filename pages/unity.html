Case study

Unity explorations in playful worldbuilding

From atmospheric prototypes to modular UI systems, these projects balance technical problem-solving with art direction inside Unity.

Worldbuilding & interaction

Experimented with lighting, particle systems, and shader graph to create reactive environments. Players trigger micro-stories that ripple through the world via custom signal systems.

Tools that empower teams

  • Editor tooling for designers to tune parameters without touching code.
  • Reusable UI kit that adapts across gamepad, keyboard, and touch input.
  • Automated build pipeline with versioned addressable assets.

Multiplayer readiness

Prototyped co-op play with Netcode for GameObjects, synchronising abilities and feedback while keeping latency forgiving. Focused on session flow, matchmaking, and shared progression states.
